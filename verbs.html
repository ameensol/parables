Power Verb List

Just because a computer might screen your resume in the beginning and look for keywords is no excuse for poor writing. Your ultimate goal is to entice a human being to read your resume, so keep the sentences interesting by using positive power verbs. Here are some great power verbs to use at the beginning of your sentences:

N | O | P | Q | R | S | T | U | V | W | Y
